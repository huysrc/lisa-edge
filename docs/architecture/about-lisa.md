# About LISA

LISA (Local Intelligent System Assistant) is an open, local-first AI ecosystem designed to become a true digital caretaker for intelligent environments.

The goal is to create an intelligent household assistant capable of understanding context, interacting naturally, coordinating smart-home devices, and operating reliably even when cloud services are unavailable.

Unlike traditional voice / cloud-dependent assistants, LISA is designed around:

- Local-first operation
- Privacy-first architecture
- Smart-home awareness
- Long-term memory
- Multi-device orchestration
- Vendor independence
- Infrastructure resilience

LISA is composed of multiple layers:

- LISA Brain
- LISA Edge
- Smart Home Infrastructure
- Future Vision and Compute Services

> LISA is not merely a chatbot or voice assistant—it is an orchestration layer capable of understanding, monitoring, reasoning, and acting across an entire environment while remaining under the owner's control.

---

## Vision

LISA aims to become an AI capable of understanding, monitoring, and assisting with everyday life inside a smart home.

Examples include:

* Natural voice conversations
* Smart home control
* Context-aware automation
* Multi-room awareness
* Presence and occupancy understanding
* Proactive notifications
* Energy optimization
* Security monitoring
* Household assistance
* Long-term memory and personalization

The system is designed to operate locally whenever possible while maintaining full control over user data.

---

## Core Principles

### Local First

Critical functionality should continue working even when Internet access is unavailable.

### Privacy First

Personal data should remain under the owner's control.

### Vendor Independence

The system should avoid unnecessary dependency on proprietary ecosystems.

### Reliability

Infrastructure should continue operating during outages, hardware replacement, and service failures.

### Extensibility

New devices, services, and AI capabilities should be easy to integrate.

## What is LISA Edge?

LISA Edge is the infrastructure layer of the LISA ecosystem.

Its purpose is to provide reliable local services that support both LISA Brain and smart-home operations.

LISA Edge is intentionally lightweight and focuses on:

* Networking
* Messaging
* Device connectivity
* Monitoring
* Backup and recovery
* Secure remote access

LISA Edge does not perform heavy AI workloads.

AI processing belongs to LISA Brain and other dedicated compute systems.
